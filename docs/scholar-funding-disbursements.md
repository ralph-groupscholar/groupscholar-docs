# Scholar Funding & Disbursements Guide

## Summary
This guide defines how Group Scholar funds scholarships, stipends, and emergency support while maintaining clear approvals, compliant payment workflows, and transparent communications with scholars and partners.

## Outcomes we care about
- Funds arrive on time with minimal friction for scholars.
- Disbursement decisions are traceable, auditable, and fair.
- Exceptions are rare, documented, and approved at the right level.
- We can answer partner questions with clean records.

## Scope
- Scholarship awards, stipends, travel grants, and emergency support.
- One-time and recurring disbursements.
- Disbursement workflows, approvals, and reconciliation.

## Roles & ownership
- Program Ops: owns award readiness, documentation, and scholar communication.
- Finance: owns payment setup, approvals, and reconciliation.
- Partner Success: aligns partner requirements, reporting cadence, and updates.
- Compliance: validates restrictions, allowable costs, and reporting needs.

## Disbursement lifecycle
1. Award readiness
   - Validate eligibility and award terms.
   - Confirm funding source, restrictions, and budget.
   - Capture required documentation and consent.
2. Scholar setup
   - Confirm payment method eligibility and tax paperwork if needed.
   - Validate contact details and preferred channels.
3. Approval gate
   - Program Ops prepares award packet and recommended schedule.
   - Finance approves payment release against budget.
4. Payment execution
   - Schedule disbursement in the payment system.
   - Notify scholar with payment timeline and support contact.
5. Reconciliation
   - Match payments to awards and update the ledger.
   - Flag exceptions and close out completed awards.

## Required documentation
- Award decision record and rubric notes.
- Funding source and restrictions summary.
- Scholar identity verification (as required by partner or policy).
- Signed consent and communications preferences.

## Disbursement schedules
- One-time awards: pay within 10 business days of approval.
- Recurring stipends: monthly or quarterly schedule with a confirmed calendar.
- Emergency support: same-day approval target when eligibility is verified.

## Exception handling
- Late payments: notify scholar within 24 hours and provide a new ETA.
- Bank or payment failures: pause and re-verify details before reissue.
- Policy exceptions: log rationale and secure executive approval.

## Partner reporting
- Monthly summary of awards, totals, and pending disbursements.
- Quarterly reconciliation against partner-funded budgets.
- Provide audit-ready logs on request.

## Scholar communication standards
- Confirm award amount, timing, and payment method.
- Share a single support point of contact.
- Document all changes or delays in the scholar record.

## Metrics to track
- Time from approval to payment.
- Payment failure rate.
- Exception frequency and root causes.
- Scholar satisfaction after disbursement.

## Risks & controls
- Segregate approval and payment release duties.
- Use standardized templates for award notifications.
- Maintain a secure, access-controlled disbursement ledger.

## Tools & systems
- Award ledger (primary source of truth).
- Payment processor dashboard.
- Shared tracker for partner-funded awards.

## Review cadence
- Monthly operational review with Finance and Program Ops.
- Quarterly policy review for partner and compliance updates.
