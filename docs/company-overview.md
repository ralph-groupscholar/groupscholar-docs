# Company Overview

## Summary
Group Scholar exists to help people build learning communities that last. This page captures our mission, values, and how we define success.

## Mission
- Make group learning accessible, structured, and joyful.
- Give facilitators the tools to run cohorts with confidence.
- Measure outcomes that show real-world progress for learners.

## Values
- Learner-first decisions
- Clear communication over cleverness
- Small experiments, fast feedback
- Durable systems over heroics

## How we measure impact
- Learner retention and completion rates
- Cohort satisfaction and referrals
- Facilitator effectiveness and repeat cohorts
- Long-term skill outcomes where available

## Key audiences
- Learners and community members
- Cohort facilitators
- Partner organizations
- Internal teams building the platform
