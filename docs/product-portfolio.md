# Product Portfolio

## Summary
Group Scholar maintains a connected product suite that supports scholar programs, partner delivery, and internal operations. This document is the living catalog of products, their purpose, and their primary users.

## Portfolio principles
- Build reusable systems that serve multiple programs
- Prioritize operational clarity over novelty
- Keep data consistent across tools and reporting
- Ship narrow tools that remove high-friction work

## Core product suite

### Program & scholar experience
- **Application Funnel**: intake and conversion tracking for program applications.
- **Scholar Experience**: service standards, triage, and scholar care playbooks.
- **Mentor Map**: mentor matching and relationship tracking.
- **Opportunity Radar**: scholarship opportunity intelligence aggregator.
- **Cohort Cadence**: touchpoint planning and cohort outreach scheduling.
- **Community Pulse**: sentiment and feedback signal tracking.

### Review & awards operations
- **Rubric Kit**: rubric sprint microsite and evaluation enablement.
- **Review Queue Forecaster**: workload projection for review cycles.
- **Review Load Balancer**: reviewer assignment smoothing and fairness checks.
- **Review Latency Lab**: cycle-time diagnostics and SLA monitoring.
- **Award Pacing Monitor**: pacing analysis for award disbursement.
- **Award Allocator**: weighted award allocation and budget integrity.

### Partner, donor, and fundraising operations
- **Partner Renewal Tracker**: renewal pipeline tracking and risk flags.
- **Donor Briefs**: fundraising brief generator for donor updates.
- **Funding Runway**: funding coverage forecasting and scenario planning.
- **Briefing Room**: weekly program update microsite for partners.

### Insights & outcomes
- **Outcome Atlas**: outcomes tracking, evidence capture, and storytelling.
- **Impact Vault**: impact intelligence library and weekly briefing builder.
- **Signal Catalog**: canonical source for operational signals and metrics.
- **Intake Auditor**: data quality checks for intake files.
- **Intake Normalizer**: CSV normalization and reporting pipeline.

### Governance & documentation
- **Group Scholar Docs**: internal operating system and playbook hub.
- **Board & Governance**: board cadence and stewardship standards.
- **Risk & Compliance**: audit readiness and compliance registry.

## Ownership & stewardship
- Each product has a named steward responsible for roadmap, health, and data integrity.
- Monthly product reviews align priorities, resourcing, and delivery risks.

## Roadmap cadence
- Quarterly portfolio review to align with program launch calendar.
- Monthly product health check across adoption, outcomes, and technical debt.

## How to update this doc
- Add new products when they launch, not when they are announced.
- Retire tools only after migration plans are complete.
- Keep descriptions to one sentence and list the primary user.
