# Customer Success & Support Guide

## Summary

This guide defines how Group Scholar keeps partners healthy, supported, and expanding. It covers ownership, support tiers, escalation, and the feedback loop that keeps product and program delivery aligned with customer goals.

## Outcomes we own

- Ensure every active partner has a clear success plan and measurable outcomes.
- Resolve support issues quickly with clear priority definitions.
- Capture product and program insights from customer feedback every week.
- Maintain a reliable renewal cadence with no surprises.

## Ownership and roles

- Success Lead: accountable for partner plans, executive updates, and renewals.
- Support Lead: owns intake, triage, and first-response quality.
- Program Manager: coordinates delivery milestones and stakeholder updates.
- Product Partner: handles roadmap alignment and escalated product gaps.

## Success plan checklist

- Document goals, key stakeholders, and success metrics.
- Agree on cadence for health checks and executive readouts.
- Define risks, dependencies, and mitigation owners.
- Confirm renewal timeline and budget checkpoints.

## Support tiers

- P0: system-wide outage or critical data loss.
- P1: core workflow blocked for a partner.
- P2: degraded experience or partial workflow impact.
- P3: general questions, requests, and low-impact issues.

## Response expectations

- P0: immediate acknowledgement, live status updates.
- P1: acknowledge within one business hour.
- P2: acknowledge within one business day.
- P3: acknowledge within two business days.

## Escalation path

- Support Lead triages and assigns within the first response window.
- Success Lead informs partner stakeholders for P0/P1 issues.
- Product Partner engaged for P0/P1 issues involving roadmap gaps.
- Engineering Lead engages if the incident requires technical mitigation.

## Feedback loop

- Capture feedback in the weekly customer insights log.
- Review top themes in the product sync every week.
- Share success highlights in monthly all-hands notes.

## Health and renewal metrics

- Health score status (green, yellow, red) per partner.
- Adoption milestones hit vs. planned timeline.
- Support volume and resolution time by priority.
- Renewal risk level and next renewal date.

## Templates to maintain

- Success plan template
- Quarterly business review outline
- Support intake form
- Escalation update notes
