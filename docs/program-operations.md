# Program Operations

## Summary
Program Operations defines how Group Scholar delivers scholarship programs day to day. It covers the operating model, lifecycle checkpoints, quality standards, and the artifacts needed to keep launches consistent.

## Purpose and scope
- Keep delivery predictable across cohorts and partners.
- Protect scholar experience with clear escalation paths.
- Ensure program teams, partners, and reviewers share the same timeline.

## Operating model
- **Program Lead:** Owns the end-to-end timeline, partner alignment, and launch readiness.
- **Operations Manager:** Runs the weekly cadence, risk log, and readiness checks.
- **Scholar Experience Lead:** Owns service standards, escalation routing, and learner care.
- **Partnerships Lead:** Aligns partner expectations, compliance, and co-marketing.
- **Analytics Lead:** Owns reporting cadence, data quality, and outcome tracking.

## Program lifecycle checkpoints
1. **Intake and scoping**
   - Confirm mission alignment, impact target, and funding coverage.
   - Lock cohort size, eligibility, and rubric criteria.
2. **Design and setup**
   - Finalize application, review workflow, and evaluation tools.
   - Publish the program calendar and reviewer commitments.
3. **Launch readiness**
   - Verify outreach plan, review staffing, and platform readiness.
   - Complete a risk review and escalation drill.
4. **Live delivery**
   - Run weekly operating reviews and scholar support huddles.
   - Track application flow, review velocity, and issue backlog.
5. **Close and handoff**
   - Deliver cohort outcomes, partner recap, and improvement plan.
   - Archive artifacts and update program templates.

## Weekly operating cadence
- **Monday pulse:** Review pipeline health, pending decisions, and risks.
- **Midweek checkpoint:** Validate review throughput and scholar support SLAs.
- **Friday recap:** Log wins, blockers, and next-week priorities.

## Quality and risk standards
- Use a readiness checklist before any public launch.
- Maintain a live risk register with owner, mitigation, and status.
- Require two-person review on final award decisions.
- Ensure scholarship communications meet brand and compliance standards.

## Partner coordination
- Confirm partner-approved communications timeline.
- Share weekly status notes with open questions and decision dates.
- Align on escalation response times and contact hierarchy.

## Reporting and handoffs
- **Internal:** Weekly program dashboard with status, risks, and priorities.
- **Partner:** Biweekly recap with metrics, stories, and open items.
- **Leadership:** Monthly impact snapshot tied to outcomes and funding.

## Core artifacts
- Program charter
- Timeline and milestone tracker
- Readiness checklist
- Risk and issue log
- Weekly status update template
- Post-program retrospective memo

## Success signals
- On-time launches with stable review throughput.
- Scholar response times within SLA targets.
- Partner satisfaction and renewal intent documented.
- Outcome reporting delivered within 30 days of cohort close.
