# Fundraising & Investor Relations

## Summary

This guide defines how Group Scholar plans, executes, and communicates fundraising so capital decisions stay aligned with mission, runway, and program impact.

## Objectives

- Maintain 18 to 24 months of runway with clear triggers for the next round.
- Align funding size and timing with program expansion, hiring, and platform milestones.
- Keep investor communication consistent, factual, and tied to impact outcomes.

## Funding strategy

- Stage focus: seed to Series A readiness with milestone-based raise timing.
- Target round size: 12 to 18 months of burn plus growth buffer.
- Non-dilutive capital: prioritize grants and strategic partners when they align with mission.

## Pipeline & outreach

- Source mix: warm intros, mission-aligned funds, program partners, and founder network.
- Weekly outreach target: 10 to 15 new contacts, 5 follow-ups.
- CRM fields: firm, partner, thesis fit, last touch, next step, probability.

## Materials checklist

- Pitch deck with mission, product, traction, and impact outcomes.
- One-pager for quick reads and warm intro forwarding.
- Data room: cap table, financial model, program metrics, legal docs.

## Diligence & governance

- Primary owner: CEO with finance lead support.
- Review committee: CEO, finance lead, and program leadership.
- Commit decision: require a documented memo with funding impact and runway math.

## Investor updates

- Monthly update cadence during active fundraising.
- Quarterly update cadence post-close.
- Update template: highlights, metrics, product roadmap, hiring, asks.

## Metrics to track

- Meetings to term sheet conversion rate.
- Days from first meeting to close.
- Runway in months and burn variance vs plan.
- Strategic value of investors (network, program access, partnerships).

## Cadence & roles

- Weekly: pipeline review and next-steps planning.
- Monthly: metrics refresh and narrative alignment.
- CEO: narrative owner and investor relationships.
- Finance lead: model owner, runway tracking, diligence support.
- Program lead: impact metrics and scholarship outcomes.
