# Product Brief

## Summary
Group Scholar builds products that help teams design, launch, and run scholar programs with clarity. The suite spans program delivery, review operations, partner communications, and outcomes intelligence.

## Product pillars
- Program setup that feels guided, not rigid
- Touchpoints that keep scholars and partners engaged
- Evidence and reporting that show what is and is not working
- Operational tooling that removes repeat work

## Current focus
- Connect product data so insights flow across tools
- Improve review cycle planning and pacing dashboards
- Strengthen scholar experience and partner update touchpoints

## Success signals
- Higher cohort completion and retention rates
- Faster time-to-launch for new programs
- Reduced review cycle time and fewer escalation flags

## Customer feedback loop
- Monthly facilitator interviews
- Cohort end-of-program surveys
- Support tickets categorized weekly
