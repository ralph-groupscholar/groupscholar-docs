# Product Brief

## Summary
Group Scholar builds tools that help facilitators design, launch, and run cohort-based learning with clarity.

## Product pillars
- Cohort setup that feels guided, not rigid
- Community touchpoints that keep learners engaged
- Insights that show what is and is not working

## Current focus
- Improve cohort onboarding and checklists
- Expand facilitator analytics dashboards
- Strengthen cohort member onboarding flows

## Success signals
- Higher cohort completion rates
- Faster time-to-launch for facilitators
- Increased learner engagement week over week

## Customer feedback loop
- Monthly facilitator interviews
- Cohort end-of-program surveys
- Support tickets categorized weekly
