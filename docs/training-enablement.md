# Training & Enablement

## Summary

Group Scholar maintains a structured training and enablement program to ensure every team member can operate confidently, serve scholars well, and keep quality high as we grow. This guide defines the learning pillars, role-based pathways, and how training content is created, maintained, and measured.

## Ownership & scope

- **Primary owner:** People Ops (enablement lead) with functional training leads in each team.
- **Scope:** Onboarding, core role proficiency, cross-functional awareness, tools/process training, and leadership growth.
- **Out of scope:** External partner training (covered in partnerships and community engagement playbooks).

## Learning pillars

- **Mission & context:** History, strategic priorities, and program outcomes.
- **Role proficiency:** The minimum competencies required to perform the role independently.
- **Process fluency:** SOPs, handoffs, escalation paths, and compliance steps.
- **Tool mastery:** Core systems, dashboards, and internal workflows.
- **Scholar-centered practice:** Empathy, cultural humility, and safeguarding norms.

## Role-based learning paths

Each team maintains a clear learning path that includes:

- **Day 0-7 quick start:** Access, baseline training, and first tasks.
- **Day 8-30 foundations:** Role SOPs, quality standards, and shadowing.
- **Day 31-60 proficiency:** Independent delivery with quality reviews.
- **Day 61-90 mastery:** Leading initiatives, process improvement, and mentoring.

## Onboarding enablement kit

- Updated role scorecard and responsibility matrix.
- Sample artifacts (case notes, comms templates, review logs).
- Recorded walkthroughs for core tools and dashboards.
- FAQs for common edge cases and escalation triggers.

## Training operations

- **Cadence:** Monthly updates for core materials, quarterly refresh for role paths.
- **Format mix:** Live sessions, recorded modules, job aids, and mentorship check-ins.
- **Versioning:** All materials include owner, last updated date, and review cadence.

## Measurement & quality

- **Readiness checks:** Short assessments for core workflows and compliance steps.
- **Shadowing outcomes:** Completion of required observations and feedback notes.
- **Performance signals:** QA scores, response timeliness, and scholar satisfaction.

## Requests & updates

- Submit training updates through the People Ops intake form.
- Include: change request, urgency, owner, and supporting evidence.
- High-impact changes are reviewed in the monthly enablement sync.
