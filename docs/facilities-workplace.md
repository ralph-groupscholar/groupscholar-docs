# Facilities & Workplace Operations

## Summary
Guidelines for managing physical and remote workspaces, vendor partners, safety protocols, and the day-to-day workplace experience that keeps teams productive and secure.

## Goals
- Provide safe, reliable workspaces for staff, scholars, and partners.
- Keep workplace costs predictable and aligned with program needs.
- Maintain clear ownership for facilities requests, vendors, and access.

## Scope
- Office space, storage, and event venues used for Group Scholar work.
- Remote work support (equipment, stipends, and workspace standards).
- Safety, access, and incident response for physical locations.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- Facilities owner: budget owner, vendor approvals, and compliance checks.
- Ops partner: intake triage, scheduling, and reporting cadence.
- Site leads: local safety checks, access logs, and onsite briefings.

## Workspace Standards
- Workspace requests include purpose, headcount, accessibility needs, and dates.
- All venues must meet accessibility and safety requirements.
- Use a standard workspace checklist (security, Wi-Fi, AV, evacuation plan).

## Vendor & Contracting Flow
- Preferred vendors are reviewed quarterly for cost and quality.
- New vendor requests require a short risk and compliance review.
- Contracts reference data handling and incident notification clauses.

## Access & Security
- Access logs must be retained for shared spaces and storage.
- Badge or key access is time-bound and reviewed monthly.
- Visitors receive a safety briefing and contact escalation list.

## Safety & Incident Response
- Post emergency contacts and evacuation routes in every workspace.
- Report incidents within 24 hours using the ops incident log.
- Conduct a quarterly safety walkthrough for active locations.

## Remote Work Support
- Maintain a standardized equipment kit and refresh cycle.
- Track stipend usage and ensure accessibility needs are met.
- Provide a single intake form for remote workspace issues.

## Metrics & Reporting
- Monthly: workspace utilization, vendor spend, and incident counts.
- Quarterly: vendor performance scorecards and safety readiness review.

## Cadence
- Weekly: workspace requests and event setup checklist review.
- Monthly: access audit and facilities spend review.
- Quarterly: vendor review and safety walkthrough recap.
